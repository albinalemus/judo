About

Judo is a modern Japanese martial art and Olympic sport that emphasizes the use of an opponent's force and balance to achieve throws and joint locks.

Judo places importance on the principles of maximum efficiency and mutual welfare. Practitioners, known as judokas, undergo rigorous training to develop physical strength, technique, and mental discipline.
